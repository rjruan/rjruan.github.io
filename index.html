About Me

As a User Interface/User Experience major at Brigham Young University-Idaho with a minor in Web Development, I am passionate about creating intuitive and impactful digital experiences. Born in Taiwan, my multicultural background enriches my approach to design and user interaction.
My academic journey is driven by a core belief: beyond meeting user requirements, it's essential to uncover and address their deeper, often unspoken needs. This philosophy fuels my high communication skills and my dedication to truly understanding and engaging with users.
In addition to my studies, I have honed my organizational and leadership abilities through my role as a school program assistant, where I effectively coordinated group projects and facilitated teamwork.
I am excited to apply my skills and knowledge to create solutions that not only meet user expectations but also exceed them by discovering their true potential needs.
